Research

I am a research assistant in Prof. Ashley Gao's research team. My research interest lies in Human-computer Interaction (HCI), Large Language Models (LLMs), and Software Engineering (SE).

Publication

Mapping the Trust Terrain: LLMs in Software Engineering - Insights and Perspectives. ICSE'25

Khati Dipin, Yijin Liu, David N. Palacio, Yixuan Zhang, and Denys Poshyvanyk

In the summer of 2024, I received a fellowship from the Charles Center to work on a project led by Professors Zhang and Poshyvanyk. The research involved exploring developers' trust in large language models (LLMs) for software engineering tasks through a combination of literature review and user studies. I had the opportunity to present our findings at the Fall Research Symposium.
